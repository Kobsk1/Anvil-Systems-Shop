WARRANTY INFORMATION

Our Ironclad warranty protection for your peace of mind

3-Year Ironclad Warranty

Every Anvil Systems build comes with our comprehensive 3-year warranty covering parts and labor. We stand behind every system we forge.

What's Covered

  • All components (CPU, GPU, RAM, Storage, PSU, Motherboard, Cooling)
  • Labor and assembly
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Replacement parts and shipping

Warranty Terms

  • 3 years from date of purchase
  • Transferable to new owner
  • No registration required
  • Coverage includes normal wear and tear

RMA Process

To initiate a warranty claim or RMA:

  1. Contact our support team at support@anvilsystems.com
  2. Provide your order number and description of the issue
  3. Our team will provide a prepaid shipping label
  4. We'll repair or replace the component within 5-7 business days

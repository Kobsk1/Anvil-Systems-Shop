FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What makes Anvil Systems different?

We hand-select every component, stress-test every build, and provide lifetime support. Every system is crafted with precision in our digital forge.

How long does it take to build my PC?

Most systems are built, tested, and shipped within 48 hours of order confirmation. Custom builds may take 3-5 business days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Free shipping on all orders over $2,000. Standard shipping is $50 for orders under $2,000.

What does the warranty cover?

Our 3-year Ironclad warranty covers all components, labor, and includes lifetime technical support. See our warranty page for full details.

Can I upgrade my system later?

Absolutely! All our systems are designed for easy upgrades. We offer upgrade services and can help you choose compatible components.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 24 hours of placement. After that, contact support for assistance.

Do you provide assembly instructions?

All systems come fully assembled and tested. If you purchase components separately, we provide detailed assembly guides and video tutorials.
